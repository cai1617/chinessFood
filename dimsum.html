Dim Sum

Dim sum refers to a style of Chinese food prepared as small bite-sized. Dim sum is also well known for the unique way it is served in some restaurants, wherein fully cooked and ready-to-serve dim sum dishes are carted around the restaurant for customers to choose their orders while seated at their tables.
