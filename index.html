Chinese cuisine

Chinese cuisine is any of several styles originating from regions of China, some of which have become increasingly popular in other parts of the world. The history of Chinese cuisine stretches back for many centuries and produced changes from period to period and in each region according to regional climate, imperial fashions,and local preferences.Major traditions include 8 different types of Chinese cuisine. They are Cantonese, Anhui, Fujian, Hunan, Jiangsu, Shandong, Szechuan, and Zhejiang cuisines.
